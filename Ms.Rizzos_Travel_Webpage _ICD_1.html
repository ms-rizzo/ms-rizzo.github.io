Travel to Japan!!

From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the quiet temples in Kyoto, there's something for everyone to see in Japan. Here are 3 reasons you're next trip should be to Japan.

1. Cherry Blossoms

Every year, from the end of March to Late April, Cherry Blossoms bloom across Japan. These beautiful flowers only blossom for two weeks out of the year, and an incredible sight to see.

2. Amazing Food

Japan offers a variety of delicious foods, each region offering their own unique style. I personally enjoyed the ramen and monjayaki most in Tokyo, oysters in Nagasaki, and sushi in Sapporo.

3. Getting Lost in Shibuya

With countless restaurants, shops, arcades, and karaoke shops, there is endless entertainment in the Shibuya area of Tokyo.
